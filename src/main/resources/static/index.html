DONE. The perfect app you need to stay productive while working from home.

Working from home can be challenging in many ways and it can be hard to get organized but there are many tools that can help you. Our task tracker is one of them. Extremely simple and focused on tracking what needs to be done and celebrating what has been done, look no further for the only app you need to keep your projects on track.

Get The App Learn More

Simple, Clean and Modern Design

Less is sometimes more and that applies to our app as well. Clean user interface that doesn't distract you and gives you the right functionality without unnecessary things you don't need.

Modern and Fully Responsive

Whatever device you're on our app will adapt so that you don't have to. Work on your tablet from you sofa continue from your phone to write down important stuff you just got from your call and enjoy full view of your agenda on your laptop when you're in office mode. Our library is continually refreshed with the latest on web technology so you'll never fall behind. Don't worry about being stuck with and old desktop app.

Web Based and Cross-Browser Compatible

Runs in every modern browser and anywhere you're online. Doesn't use too much data so even if you're stuck on a desert island, you'll be ok. No updates for you to care about - our app handles that automatically and stays up to date.

Advanced Filtering Options

Not only you can track what has been done and what is in backlog, you can assign your task to a person and also specify a building on which you need this task to be completed. Tasks then can be filtered so that you know exactly who is working on what and no task gets left behind.
